About Us

SAUVI UGANDA is a community-driven organization dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty among children and youth at the grassroots level. Founded in 2021, we were first registered with Wakiso District in 2022. Today, we operate across four districts: Wakiso, Mityana, Kassanda, and Mubende.

Vision

Breaking down the shackles of poverty among children and youth.

Mission

Investing in the lives of children and youth, supporting them build a health environment, allowing them to thrive and create a lasting change within self and the community at large.

What We Do

Education Support

Providing children with access to quality education.

Economic Empowerment

Creating sustainable opportunities for youth and communities.
